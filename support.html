Lifetime Bible — Support

Lifetime Bible is an offline Bible reader with verse-pinned notes, word-level highlighting, search, Kindle/notes import, study layers, a friend circle, and cloud backup.

Need help?

Email hello@jventerprises.app and we’ll get back to you.

Common questions

Do I need an account? No — the Bible and your own notes work fully offline. An account only adds cloud backup and sharing with friends.

How do I back up my notes? Sign in (Settings → Account & Friend Circle). You can also export everything from Settings → Your notes.

How do I delete my account? Settings → Account & Friend Circle → Delete my account. This permanently removes your account and all cloud data.
