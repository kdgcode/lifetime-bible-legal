Lifetime Bible — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

What we collect

If you create an account, we store your email address (to sign you in) and the content you create — your notes and highlights — so they can back up to the cloud and sync to your other devices. The app works fully without an account; if you never sign in, your notes stay only on your phone.

AI import (optional)

If you use the AI-assisted import or the “photograph my notes” feature, the highlighted text or photo you choose is sent securely to our AI provider (Anthropic) only to match it to Bible verses or transcribe it. It is not used to train AI models.

What we never do

We don’t sell your data, show ads, or track you across other apps. Your private notes are visible only to you. Notes you mark “public” are shared only with people in your own friend circle or groups.

Deleting your data

You can permanently delete your account and all cloud data anytime from Settings → Account & Friend Circle → Delete my account.
